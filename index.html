About Me

 

Simon is an ordinary person born and raised in Macau (the former and the last colony of Portugal). He is a first-generation college student in his family. He traveled to Beijing in 2011 and started studying mathematics since then. In 2015, he came to Hong Kong to continue his journey of PhD in mathematics. His career started in College Station TX. He aims at understanding the real and yet complex world, on which he desires to make an impact, through his mathematical knowledge and expertise. Now he is working on Quantum Chemistry and Density Functional Theory, and their combinations with Machine Learning.
